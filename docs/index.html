Privacy

"Privacy is one of the biggest problems in this new electronic age." - Andy Grove

Have you ever wondered if somebody could be reading your emails or text messages? Fret not, for CryptoAlgo comes to the rescue! Worry no more, for CryptoAlgo allows you to encrypt your messages with secure AES-256 encryption and RSA encryption. What's more, all of these is wrapped in an easy-to-use intcommand line app that has a vast array of customizability options.

3 Simple Steps

1. Key in text

Simpily key in the text that you wish to encrypt, and copy the encrypted text.

2. Encrypt the AES keyfiles

Encrypt the AES keyfiles with the other party's RSA public key with the inbuilt, easy to use tools.

3. Send the encrypted output

Send the encrypted text produced in step 1 with the encrypted AES keyfiles to the other party.

Encryption and decryption of files [Alpha]

Sending confidential files? No problem!

This experimental function allows you to easily and securely encrypt or decrypt files with the same security as encrypting text messages. Don't worrk, with our accelerated development timeline, this feature will be in stable builds in no time!

US Military Grade Encryption employed

Be assured that your files and texts are safe.

Two of the world's most secure encryption standards, AES and RSA, are employed to securely encrypt your files and texts. The application has been tested multiple times to have no security loopholes.
